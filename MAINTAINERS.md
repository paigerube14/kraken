MAINTAINERS
This document outlines the roles and responsibilities of the various contributors to this project.

Contributor Ladder
This project follows a contributor ladder model, where contributors can take on more responsibilities as they gain experience and demonstrate their commitment to the project. The roles are:

Member: A contributor who is active in the community.

Reviewer: A contributor who reviews pull requests.

Approver: A contributor who can approve pull requests for merging.

Maintainer: A contributor who is responsible for the overall health and direction of the project.

Owner: A contributor who has administrative ownership of the project.
